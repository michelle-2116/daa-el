# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Vaccine logistics operators and fleet dispatchers monitoring cargo health and routing in real-time.

## Product Purpose
Real-time tracking of vaccine transport temperatures, routes, and risk factors with dynamic rerouting recommendations to protect cargo viability.

## Brand Personality
Clean, corporate-professional, high clarity, and clinical. Designed to feel like a premium medical/logistics control center.

## Anti-references
Gimmicky consumer-facing interfaces with excessive decorative elements, large playful illustrations, or overly rounded corners.

## Design Principles
- Clinical Clarity: Information is structured for instant scannability; temperature alerts cannot be missed.
- Actionable Routing: Rerouting recommendations are clearly compared with the original path with clear cost-benefit metrics.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear high-contrast layouts suitable for quick scanning under varying warehouse/field lighting conditions.
